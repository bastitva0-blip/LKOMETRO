Privacy Policy

LkoMetro · Last updated June 2026

LkoMetro does not represent a government entity. Timings and fares are sourced from the official UPMRC website.

What we collect

LkoMetro is designed to work fully offline and stores almost everything locally on your device. We do not run user accounts, and we do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.

Local storage

Your GoSmart Card preference and whether you've seen the welcome screen are stored in your browser's local storage, on your device only. This data is never transmitted to us.

Location

If you use a feature that requests your location (e.g. nearest station search), your coordinates are used only within your browser to calculate distances and are not sent to any server we operate.

Map tiles

Map imagery is loaded from OpenStreetMap tile servers. Standard web request metadata (such as your IP address) is visible to OpenStreetMap's infrastructure when tiles are fetched, per their own privacy practices.

Analytics

We do not currently use any third-party analytics or advertising trackers.
